Location

We will be dancing at Centre Saint-Barthélemy, 7111 avenue des Érables, Montréal. This fabulous and recently renovated building is just steps from the D'Iberville metro station.

We recommend taking the metro to and from the hall. D'Iberville station is just around the corner, and trains operate every 10 minutes or better all weekend. Buy an Unlimited Weekend pass!
